About CCA iGEM

iGEM, short for International Genetically Engineered Machine, is a synthetic biology competition for high schoolers, undergraduates, graduate students, and entrepreneurs, in which teams develop SynBio solutions to problems in todayâ€™s society. Students work to display their leadership, interest in the community, inclusivity, and entrepreneurship.

In 2021, our team ran the iGEM Science Camp online and wrote a book about Biochemistry that we published to Amazon, winning the Best Education award. We also received a gold medal overall and were among the top 10 high school teams worldwide. In 2022, we ran our first successful in-person camp after quarantine!
