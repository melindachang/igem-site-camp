Camp Information

We aspire to provide an engaging and interactive experience for motivated students who are interested in pursuing a STEM career. We are very excited to welcome you to our in-person camp this summer!

About the Camp

This is the fifth annual science summer camp hosted by CCA’s iGEM team! We teach advanced pre-high school students who are interested in pursuing a STEM career in the future, or want to explore different possible career options. We are very excited for this year’s iGEM camp because it’s the first in-person camp in two years. The past two had been online because of the pandemic.

Our camp will teach many different important science topics such as biology, synthetic biology, chemistry, and forensics, as well as have daily labs that will reinforce what the students learned. The content we teach will not only prepare students for high school-level courses such as biology and chemistry, but it will also help their application should they wish to be a part of CCA’s iGEM team in high school.

Logistics

When does the camp take place?

We will be holding two sessions, one from June 13-17th, and one from August 1-5th. Daily times will be from 9 am to 2 pm, which includes a lunch and snack break.

Where will the camp be held?

We expect this year's camp to be held in-person at Canyon Crest Academy, but due to the unpredictability of COVID, we cannot guarantee that the camp will be in-person. If we need to move to an online format, we will notify all parents and give a full refund for those who are unwilling to attend the camp online.

How much does it cost to attend?

$350 early bird, $400 after May 10th. Tuition will pay for the fun labs we prepare every day, as well as help us enroll in the iGEM competition in October. Tuition will not be refunded if our camp stays in-person. Camp attendees who refer the camp to other students will get a $20 discount off of their tuition, so please spread the word!

Is lunch provided?

Lunch is not included. Students should bring their own lunches daily, which can be eaten during the lunch break. Students may also bring a snack.
